Overview

Purpose

The website will convey the fun we can have on the river, times of year we run, sell the supplies people need, and promote river running as a whole. The entire family needs to be advertised as having fun and that it is a safe activity for all no matter if they are fit or handicapped. One day and two day trips will be the most advertised as well as week long trips will be advertised for the more adventurous individuals. The main message of our site will be: “Rafting is a fun activity for all to enjoy. Come and experience it for yourself and bring your family.”

Audience

Target audience:

  • Adventurous
  • Mid 20's to 50
  • Upper middle class to upper class income
  • healthy lifestyle
  • family oriented for one and two day trips
  • Single professionals for week long trips What do they want?
  • These people want a fun adventure to share with family or friends/co-workers
  • They want to experience something new to them
  • They want to re-experience the adrenaline rush you get while rafting. Needs to being met?
  • By outdoor supplies relating to water activities
  • Want a fun and safe way to satisfy an adrenalin rush What devices will be use to access the site?

The site has to be desktop and mobile friendly. If it does not work on cell phones and tablets, Google will not give it high rankings in the SERP.
