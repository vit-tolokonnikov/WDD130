I want to learn more

Connect those around you

1. Gather like-minded people First, tell your neighbors about your idea. Some may refuse, others will accept the idea with joy. Of course, you can set up separate waste collection in your home on your own, but the more residents, the easier it is. As a rule, when everything is ready and the system is adjusted, the rest are pulled up.

2. We study the services of companies for the separate collection of waste Waste collection today is carried out by commercial organizations that independently export recyclable materials to processing plants. Try contacting these companies to inquire about services and rates. You can find them in several ways.

  • Search companies on the Internet for "buy/sell recyclables" and contact them.
  • Ask janitors and local stores. Most of the time they are aware.
  • Write to environmental movements and initiative groups in your city.
  • Contact local authorities.

Plastic

Sorting plastic is easy enough. Almost every package necessarily has a corresponding marking - a small triangle with a number. Therefore, we look at it and sort it. Before sorting, it is necessary to wash each plastic bottle or container, remove the film and stickers, if possible, crush to reduce the volume. Vegetable oil bottles, polystyrene foam and products with other markings are not accepted. Unfortunately, these types of waste will have to be sent to a regular trash can or unsorted waste container.

Paper

There is a general list of paper suitable for recycling. It can be found on the Internet or check at the reception point. To hand over waste paper, it is necessary to separate metal elements (springs, paper clips) and plastic elements (covers, files, folders). If possible, compress the paper to reduce the volume. Do not hand over paper in plastic bags, it is enough to tie a pile with a rope or put it in a box.

Metal

Almost any product can be handed over as a metal container: aluminum or tin cans, unpainted aluminum foil, can lids and small household scrap metal. Not everywhere accepts aerosol cans, foil and disposable baking containers. Before delivery, all containers must be freed from food debris, remove the lids, remove the labels and crumple the jars.

Glass

Bottles, jars and broken glass are considered glass containers. All this, too, must be cleaned of food residues and remove the covers. It is not necessary to remove the labels. Most likely, you will not accept light bulbs, ceramic and porcelain dishes, pan lids, optical or automotive glass, crystal. But it depends on the reception point, so you should check in advance
